# Skills vs SaaS

## Purpose
Alege rapid intre skill custom si SaaS pe baza cost/valoare/risc.

## Instructions
1. Evalueaza criteriile:
   - cost recurent
   - flexibilitate
   - privacy
   - time-to-build
   - maintenance/compliance
   - reliability.
2. Aplica regula de aur: daca SaaS > 20 USD/luna si un skill acopera >= 80% in < 4 ore, prefera skill.
