# Apply the Outline Expansion Pattern

[AI-PROMPT-ENGINEERING] Apply the Outline Expansion Pattern — Procedura Standard de Operare
Scope: Builds complex long-form content by first establishing a structural outline and then expanding each section individually, maintaining consistency and allowing course-correction at the structure level before investing in full content generation.
Steps:
- Generate the High-Level Outline
- Expand the Outline to Second Level
- Establish Cross-Section Consistency Rules
- Expand Sections Individually
- Compile and Check for Consistency
- Write Introduction and Conclusion Last
