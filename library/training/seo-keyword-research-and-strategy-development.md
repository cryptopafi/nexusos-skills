# Keyword Research and Strategy Development

[SEO] Keyword Research and Strategy Development — Procedura Standard de Operare
Scope: Structured keyword research process from seed keyword identification through topic clustering, intent mapping, and content gap prioritization to build a data-driven keyword universe for organic strategy.
Steps:
- Define Seed Keywords and Business Context
- Expand Keyword Universe with Research Tools
- Filter by Search Intent
- Cluster Keywords by Topic
- Prioritize by Keyword Difficulty vs. Search Volume
- Map Keywords to Pages and Identify Gaps
- Deliver Keyword Strategy Document
