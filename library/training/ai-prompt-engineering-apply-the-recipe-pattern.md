# Apply the Recipe Pattern

[AI-PROMPT-ENGINEERING] Apply the Recipe Pattern — Procedura Standard de Operare
Scope: Prompts the AI to generate a complete, ordered, actionable plan from a defined goal and known constraints — analogous to a recipe that specifies all steps and ingredients needed to reach the desired output.
Steps:
- Define the Desired Output Clearly
- Specify Known Ingredients and Constraints
- Request the Recipe
- Validate Recipe Completeness
- Test the Recipe on a Small Scale
- Execute, Document Deviations, and Update Recipe
