Self-Improvement Cascade v2.1 — UPDATED with Self-Grading Phase

Ciclul complet (7 pasi):

FAZA 1: RESEARCH (Delphi Discovery) — multi-source search, min 5 tipuri surse, source routing
FAZA 2: INGEST (ECHELON) — ingestie masiva in Cortex, tag per source/type/tier
FAZA 3: INSIGHT (Opus) — analiza categorized: PROCEDURE/SKILL/PLUGIN/INFO
FAZA 3.5: SELF-GRADING (NOU v2.1) — benchmark intern, scor 0-100 pe 5 dimensiuni
FAZA 4: STANDARDIZE — proceduri LEGO, Cortex, implementare
FAZA 5: RADAR — monitoring zilnic surse existente
FAZA 6: DELPHI RE-DISCOVERY — lunar, surse noi

SELF-GRADING (Faza 3.5) — 5 dimensiuni, fiecare 0-20:
1. PRECISION: queries precise? surse gasite vs surse utile. 20=zero noise
2. COVERAGE: toate tipurile de surse acoperite? 20=complete
3. DEPTH: material inteles, nu doar colectat? 20=papers citite integral
4. NOVELTY: cat e NOU vs deja cunoscut? 20=paradigm shift
5. ACTIONABILITY: insights implementabile concret? 20=toate cu steps clari

Interpretare: 85-100 Excellent, 70-84 Good, 50-69 Acceptable, sub50 Poor (re-run)

Obligatoriu:
- Delta per dimensiune vs ciclul anterior
- COMPOUND CHECK: Ciclu N a beneficiat masurabil de Ciclu N-1? Da/Nu + evidenta
- Scor salvat in Cortex metadata per ciclu pentru tracking longitudinal
